What a student MADE Record includes

MADE Record

Materials
Assignment brief, final work, drafts, sources, notes, versions, and AI conversations
Actions
Researching, prompting, drafting, checking, editing, and revising
Decisions
Choices about sources, structure, content, and how AI output was used
Evolution
How the assignment changed from early work to the final submission

Why this helps

A final assignment does not show how it was made.

A teacher may need more than the final file, while a long raw AI chat is difficult to review.

A MADE Record brings the student’s materials, actions, decisions, and development into one short report.
