When this helps

A final assignment does not show how it was made.

A teacher may need more than the final file, but a long raw AI chat is difficult to review.

A short record shows the main decisions and materials in one place.

What goes in

Use the materials you already have.

The record is based on materials provided by the student. It helps explain the process, but it is not independent proof and does not guarantee acceptance or a grade.

You provide

  • assignment brief and final file
  • AI chats or prompts
  • outline, drafts, and notes
  • sources, screenshots, or version history

The record shows

  • what you decided
  • how AI helped
  • what drafts and sources exist
  • what still needs teacher review
