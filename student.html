What you send / what you get

Turn scattered school materials into one short record.

The record helps explain the process. It does not promise that a teacher will accept the work.

You can include

  • assignment brief
  • final file
  • AI chats or prompts
  • outline, drafts, notes, version history

The record shows

  • what you decided
  • how AI helped
  • what evidence exists
  • what the record cannot prove

When this helps

Your teacher may not want only the final file.

They may also want to understand how you worked.

A short record can show your role and your AI use in a simple way.

What the record can show

Clear process context for a teacher.

The record is not a grade decision. It is a short explanation of the student process.

Assignment title what work is being reviewed

What this is not

Honest process sharing, not a shortcut around rules.

Not a cheating detector Not an automatic grade decision Not academic certification Not legal certification Not proof of originality Not a punishment system

FAQ

A review aid, not an automatic judgment.

The record is based on materials the student provides. It does not decide whether a teacher should accept the work, assign a grade, or make an academic integrity decision.

It only helps show the work process in a clear, short format.
