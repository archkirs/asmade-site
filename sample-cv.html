About the work

The analysed Work is a two-page bilingual professional CV in English and Serbian.

The Record focuses on how the text was prepared and reviewed. It does not independently verify the professional claims contained in the CV.

How it was made

Ian Kirs states that he supplied the underlying professional facts about his experience, education and skills.

ChatGPT was used to help collect, structure, rewrite and propose wording for the English CV based on information supplied by Ian.

The available process record also shows an English CV text being translated into Serbian through ChatGPT. Ian then requested checking and corrections to the Serbian version.

The final CV existed as an editable two-page Canva document before export to PDF.

Human contribution

  • supplying the underlying professional information, according to Ian's declaration;
  • reviewing and directing the wording process;
  • requesting Serbian translation and later corrections;
  • actively reviewing the Serbian text;
  • approving the final CV, according to Ian's declaration.

AI assistance

  • structuring and rewriting English CV content, according to Ian's declaration;
  • proposing wording;
  • translating the English CV into Serbian;
  • assisting with checking and correcting the Serbian wording.

The available archive does not establish that these were the only AI uses.

How the work evolved

The clearest recorded process sequence is:

English CV text→AI-assisted Serbian translation→Human-requested checking and correction

The complete earlier drafting history is not available. The Record therefore does not claim to reconstruct every draft or identify the origin of every line.

What remains uncertain

The original factual-input messages and earlier English structuring/rewriting conversations are not currently located as controlled evidence.

The complete draft history and exact line-by-line human/AI attribution are not established.

The Record does not independently verify the professional claims contained in the CV. The exact ChatGPT model/version and complete Canva edit/export history are also not established.
