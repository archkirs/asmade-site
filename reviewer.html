The review problem

A final file shows too little. A long AI chat shows too much.

A MADE Record gives the reviewer a readable starting point.

The creator provides the materials, checks the generated record, and shares it with the reviewer.

What the reviewer receives

One short record with the main review questions.

SectionWhat it answers
MaterialsWhich drafts, files, conversations, versions, sources, or screenshots were submitted
ActionsWhich supported steps the person took during the work
DecisionsWhat the person planned, selected, changed, rejected, and approved
EvolutionHow the work developed across drafts, versions, and feedback
Evidence status and open questionsWhat is declared, supported, uncertain, or still needs follow-up

How it works

A clearer review process.

1

Invite a creator

Ask for a MADE Record for a specific piece of work.

2

The creator uploads materials

The creator provides the final work and relevant process materials.

3

AsMade prepares a draft

The creator checks and approves the structured MADE Record.

4

Review and follow up

Read the record and request more detail when questions remain.
