AsMade for Reviewers

Understand how the work was made before making your own judgment.

The finished work shows the result. But it does not always make clear what the person did, where AI was used, or which decisions shaped the outcome.

A MADE Record brings that context together around one specific work.

It helps show the human and AI roles, important actions and decisions, available process information, and what remains unknown.

iA MADE Record gives you more context for your evaluation. It does not make the decision for you.

The result is visible. Its origin is not always.

AI use may be an important part of the work. But that fact alone explains very little.

In one work, AI may have been used only to check text. In another, to generate source images. In a third, for research, translation, or a first draft.

At the same time, important decisions, selection, editing, checking, and responsibility may still have remained with the person. The finished result does not always make that difference clear.

A MADE Record helps show not just that AI was used, but where it fit into the specific process of creating the work.

Different sources show different parts of the work

An AI chat is a source of information, not the complete creation history.

The work may also include research, drafts, source files, other software, generation of alternatives, selection, editing, checking, and decisions made outside the AI chat.

That is why a long AI conversation does not always give a complete picture of the work. A MADE Record helps connect the important information around one specific result.

Main first. Detail when needed.

You do not need to start with the full archive of materials.

Record

Start with the work itself, the people connected to it, the main findings, and important limitations.

Process

Then review the main stages of creation and the roles of the person and AI.

Evidence

When needed, see which materials individual statements rely on and which materials are available.

History

See the Record version and meaningful changes over time.

This order lets you understand the main picture first and move into detail only when it is actually useful.

How it works

From a specific work to a clearer basis for evaluation.

1

Identify the specific work

The MADE Record is linked to a defined work and version.

2

Review available information

Files, versions, drafts, notes, AI chats, and other materials connected to creating the work may be reviewed when available.

3

Create the MADE Record

AsMade brings together the main human and AI roles, important actions and decisions, available materials, and significant limitations.

4

You review the Record

Start with the main picture and move to more detailed information when needed.

5

You make your own decision

The MADE Record adds context. It does not decide quality, acceptability, truth, or value for you.

Different information, different basis

Not every statement in a MADE Record has the same level of support.

Creator-provided information

Some information may come from the creator's own account.

Directly observable information

Some information may be visible in available files or work history.

Analysis across materials

Some conclusions may be based on several available materials.

Open questions

Some questions may remain unanswered.

A MADE Record should make these differences clear rather than hiding them behind a simple “verified” label.

Materials are context, not an automatic verdict

The presence or absence of one material does not decide the question by itself.

An available file may support one action or stage. An AI chat may show one interaction with the model. A document version may show one change.

But one material does not necessarily explain the whole process. And the absence of a particular material does not, by itself, mean misconduct, deception, or bad faith.

A MADE Record helps show what is known, what that understanding is based on, and where limitations remain.

Context for your own decision

One Record can be useful in different situations.

A MADE Record may be reviewed by teachers, clients, editors, publishers, employers, contests, platforms, professional communities, and other people or organisations that need to understand the origin and creation process of a specific work.

A MADE Record does not replace their own rules, criteria, or procedures.

What a MADE Record does not do

More context. Not a ready-made verdict.

A MADE Record is not an AI detector, legal certificate, proof of authorship, proof of copyright or ownership, a guarantee that every material was provided, proof that every statement inside the work is true, or a universal decision about quality or acceptability.

AsMade helps make the origin and creation process of a work easier to understand. The final evaluation remains with the person or organisation using the Record.
