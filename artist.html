Why this can matter

AI may be part of the work. That still does not explain what the person did.

One creator may use AI only for ideas. Another may generate source images and then spend substantial time selecting, editing, composing, and refining them. Another may use AI for text, translation, or research.

A MADE Record helps make the actual human and AI roles in one specific work easier to understand.

What a MADE Record can show

The creative role behind the finished result.

QuestionWhat the Record can clarify
What was the creative direction?The intended work, goals, and choices that shaped the project
What did the person do?Writing, creating, selecting, editing, composing, assembling, changing, checking, and other human work
Where was AI used?The tasks AI supported and how AI output influenced or entered the final work
Which decisions mattered?Accepted, rejected, changed, or approved options and the choices that shaped the result
What information is available?Drafts, source files, versions, AI chats, images, notes, references, and other available process materials

The missing context

The final work and the AI chat show different parts of the story.

The finished work shows the result, but may not make the human contribution and AI role clear.

An AI chat can show part of the interaction with AI, but not necessarily work done in other tools, source files, manual edits, selection, composition, or decisions made outside the chat.

A MADE Record connects the important context around the specific work in one place.

Context for other people

Useful when someone needs to understand how the work was created.

A MADE Record may help a client, editor, publisher, employer, platform, contest, professional community, audience, or another person better understand the origin and creation of a work.

It does not tell them how they must judge the work. It gives them additional context for their own decision.

How it works

From one creative work to a clearer account of how it was made.

1

Identify the work

The MADE Record is linked to a specific creative work and version.

2

Review available information

Drafts, source files, versions, notes, AI chats, references, and other relevant materials may be considered when available.

3

Create and check the Record

AsMade organises the human and AI roles, key decisions, available support, and important limits. The creator can check factual information about their own work.

4

Others read and decide

Readers can start with the main result and go deeper into process and evidence only when they need to.

The MADE structure

A deeper way to organise information about the creative process.

MADECreative-work context
MaterialsThe final work, sketches, drafts, versions, source files, notes, references, AI chats, and other available materials
ActionsDeveloping ideas, using AI, creating, selecting, editing, composing, checking, and revising
DecisionsCreative direction, accepted and rejected options, changes, and final choices
EvolutionHow the work changed from early ideas to the final result, when that history is available

Limits

More context. Not proof of authorship or ownership.

A MADE Record can make the human role, AI use, and available creation history easier to understand. Missing process material does not automatically mean deception, and the Record does not decide legal rights, quality, or creative value.
