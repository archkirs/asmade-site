The problem

The final creative work does not show the decisions behind it.

When AI was used, a client, editor, publisher, or audience may misunderstand the human role.

The record shows the decisions, selections, and edits that came from you.

What goes in

Show the process behind the finished work.

The record is based on materials provided by the person who made the work. It helps review the process, but it is not independent proof.

You provide

  • final image, text, design, or comic page
  • AI chats or prompts
  • sketches, drafts, and versions
  • notes, references, and source files

The record shows

  • your idea and direction
  • your selections and edits
  • how AI helped
  • what materials and open questions exist
