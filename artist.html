Why this helps

The finished work does not show the decisions behind it.

When AI was used, a client, editor, publisher, contest, or audience may misunderstand the human role.

A MADE Record shows the materials, actions, decisions, and evolution behind the finished work.

What the record shows

Connect the finished work to the creative process.

You provideThe MADE Record shows
Final image, text, design, manuscript, or other workThe result and review context
Sketches, drafts, versions, notes, and source filesActions, creative direction, development, selections, and edits
AI conversations or promptsWhat AI supported and how its output was used
References and production materials, when availableWhat is supported and what remains uncertain
