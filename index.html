Materials

Open questions
What the reviewer still needs to decide

The problem

The final file shows the result, not the process.

Drafts, notes, version history, and AI conversations may be spread across different tools.

An HCR brings the main information into one short report without pretending to make the final decision.

Clear about what is known

A useful record should be clear about what it can and cannot show.

Declared by the creator

Information the person provides about their own process and AI use.

Supported by submitted materials

Statements connected to drafts, files, version history, screenshots, or AI conversations.

Still needs reviewer judgment

Questions the record cannot decide for a teacher, client, editor, or other reviewer.
