About

A final file does not show the work behind it.

AI tools can help with research, writing, design, structure, editing, images, and revisions. But when someone sees only the final result, they may not know what the human actually did.

A Human Contribution Record organizes submitted materials into a short reviewable summary: human contribution, AI assistance, process evidence, digital provenance, and limitations.

How it works

Share

Shows planning, editing, selection, rejection, direction, and final approval.

AI assistance
Shows which tools were used and what they helped with.

Process evidence
Organizes drafts, notes, prompts, screenshots, versions, and references.

Limitations
States what the record does not prove and what still requires review.

FAQ

Evidence of process, not automatic judgment.

The pilot is based on submitted materials only. It does not independently determine legal ownership, copyright status, publication rights, academic acceptance, platform compliance, or originality.

In the current pilot, records are created manually. Do not submit confidential materials until invited to a controlled pilot.
