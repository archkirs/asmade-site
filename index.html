The problem

The final file shows what was made. Not what made it.

Drafts, notes, version history, and AI conversations may be spread across different tools.

A MADE Record brings the useful process context into one short report without pretending to make the final decision.

Clear about what is known

A useful record should show where each statement comes from.

Declared by the creator

Information the person provides about their process and AI use.

Supported by submitted materials

Statements connected to drafts, files, version history, screenshots, or AI conversations.

Still needs reviewer judgment

Questions AsMade cannot decide for a teacher, client, editor, or other reviewer.
