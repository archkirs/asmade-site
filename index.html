What AsMade creates

One work. One record of how it was made.

A MADE Record is linked to one specific work. It helps make the most important creation context easier to understand.

Who was involved?

The people or roles connected to creating the work.

What did the person do?

The actions, choices, edits, and decisions that shaped the result.

Where was AI used?

Which parts AI supported and how its output was used in the work.

What is the account based on?

Available drafts, files, versions, AI chats, notes, and other process materials.

What remains unknown?

Information that is missing, uncertain, or cannot be established from what is available.

The missing context

The result is visible. The human contribution and the role of AI are not always.

Finished work

Shows what was produced. But it does not always make clear who made the key decisions or what role AI played.

AI chat

May show part of the interaction with AI. But the work may also include research, drafts, files, other tools, selection, editing, and decisions made outside the chat.

MADE Record

Brings the important context around one specific work into one place. It does not try to show absolutely everything; it helps people understand the creation story and what that account is based on.

How it works

From a specific work to clearer context.

1

Identify the specific work

Each MADE Record is linked to a defined work and version.

2

Review available information

Drafts, files, versions, notes, AI chats, and other materials connected to the work may be reviewed when available.

3

Create the MADE Record

AsMade brings together the main human and AI roles, important actions and decisions, available materials, and significant limitations.

4

The reader reviews and decides

Start with the main picture, then go deeper into process and available materials when needed. The final judgment remains with the person.

More about the structure

MADE helps organise information about how the work was created.

MADE is four ways of looking at the process.

MADEWhat it helps organise
MaterialsThe work itself and available drafts, files, notes, sources, versions, AI chats, and other materials
ActionsResearching, creating, using AI, selecting, editing, checking, and revising
DecisionsWhat was accepted, changed, rejected, or approved, and how those choices shaped the result
EvolutionHow the work changed over time, when that history is available

MADE is not a rigid template of four mandatory, equally complete parts. It is a way to make process information easier to understand.

More context, not a ready-made verdict

Not all information has the same basis.

A MADE Record can make visible:

What the creator says

Information provided by the creator about the work and process.

What connects to available materials

Statements linked to drafts, files, versions, AI chats, notes, or other materials.

What follows from analysis

Conclusions drawn from the available information.

What remains unresolved

Information that is unsupported, incomplete, uncertain, or unknown.

AsMade does not hide these differences behind a simple “verified” label.

What AsMade does not do

More information. The decision remains yours.

A MADE Record is not an AI detector, legal certificate, proof of copyright or ownership, proof that a work was created entirely by a person, or a guarantee that the content of the work is true or correct.

AsMade helps people better understand the origin and creation of a work. It does not make the final decision for you.

Not an AI detectorNot legal certificationNot copyright or ownership proofNot proof of fully human authorshipNot a truth guarantee
