Why context matters

The result is visible. The human contribution and the role of AI may not be.

A finished work tells you what was produced. An AI chat may show only one part of the process.

A MADE Record connects the important context around one specific work so you do not have to reconstruct the story from scattered files, chats, drafts, and versions.

What a MADE Record can help answer

The questions that matter when the result alone is not enough.

What did the person do?

Actions, choices, edits, checks, and decisions connected to the human role.

Where did AI help?

The tasks AI supported and how AI output affected the final work.

What is still unknown?

Missing, incomplete, uncertain, or unavailable parts of the creation history.

How it works

From one specific work to a clearer account of how it was made.

1

Identify the work

Each MADE Record is linked to a specific work and version.

2

Review available information

Drafts, files, versions, notes, AI chats, and other relevant materials may be considered when available.

3

Create the MADE Record

AsMade organises the key human and AI roles, decisions, supporting information, and important limits.

4

Read and decide for yourself

Start with the main result, then go deeper into process and evidence only when you need to.

The MADE structure

A simple way to organise deeper process information.

MADEWhat it helps organise
MaterialsThe work itself and available drafts, files, notes, sources, versions, AI chats, and other materials
ActionsResearching, creating, using AI, selecting, editing, checking, and revising
DecisionsWhat was chosen, changed, rejected, or approved and how those choices shaped the result
EvolutionHow the work changed over time, when that history is available

MADE is a presentation structure, not a claim that every work has four equally complete parts.

Clear about the basis

Not every statement has the same support.

What the creator says

Information provided by the person about their work and process.

What available materials support

Statements connected to files, drafts, versions, screenshots, AI conversations, or other available information.

What still needs judgment

Questions that remain uncertain, incomplete, disputed, or outside what the Record can decide.

Limits

More context. Not a ready-made verdict.

A MADE Record helps people understand the origin and creation of a work. It does not guarantee that the content is true, correct, original, or legally owned by a particular person.

Not an AI detectorNot legal certificationNot copyright or ownership proofNot proof of fully human authorshipDoes not replace human judgment
