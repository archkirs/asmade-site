The information gap

The final work is visible. The path to it often isn't.

AI-assisted work can leave the useful context spread across the finished file, AI chats, drafts, screenshots, notes and memory. None of those sources alone necessarily tells the whole story.

Finished work

Shows the result. It may not reveal who made the key choices or how AI output was used.

01
Raw AI chat

Shows one tool interaction. It can miss edits, offline decisions, other tools, selection and assembly.

02
Ad-hoc evidence

Drafts, screenshots and notes can contain the answer, but the reader still has to piece the process together.

03
MADE Record

Organises the important context around one identifiable work, links findings to available support, and keeps uncertainty visible.

Trust without a verdict

A MADE Record shows its basis and its limits. It does not decide for you.

One identifiable Work

Each Record refers to a defined work and version rather than a vague person-level score.

Support stays differentiated

Creator-provided information, linked materials, analysis and unresolved questions are not flattened into one “verified” badge.
